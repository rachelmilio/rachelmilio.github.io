suit for ejectment
Definition: Private lawsuit for physically obstructing prosecutor from inheritance, defendant summoned by plaintiff via prosklesis, initiated by submitting a charge in writing to magistrate, supervised by Eponymous Archon, tried by the dikasterion, has punishment for convicted of restore property to prosecutor and pay cash value to state, has penalty of epobelia for failed prosecution, with non-assessable penalty.
hypernym(s): non-assessable lawsuit (preferred), private lawsuit (preferred),
Status: preferred
Equivalent(s):
