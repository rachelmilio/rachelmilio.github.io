indictment for illegal proposals
Definition: Indictment for proposing or enacting an illegal law, supervised by the Thesmothetai, tried by the dikasterion, with assessable penalty through timesis, has punishment for convicted of a penal assessment.
hypernym(s): assessable lawsuit (preferred), indictment (preferred),
Status: preferred
