suit for theft
Definition: Private lawsuit for theft of private property, defendant summoned by plaintiff via prosklesis, initiated by submitting a charge in writing to magistrate, supervised by the Eleven, tried by the dikasterion, has punishment for convicted of double damages, has no penalty for failed prosecution, with assessable penalty through timesis.
hypernym(s): assessable lawsuit (preferred), private lawsuit (preferred),
Status: preferred
