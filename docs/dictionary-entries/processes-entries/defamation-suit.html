defamation suit
Definition: Private lawsuit for illegal defamatory speech, defendant summoned by plaintiff via prosklesis, initiated by submitting a charge in writing to magistrate, supervised by the Forty, tried by the dikasterion, has punishment for convicted of fixed fine, has penalty of epobelia for failed prosecution, with non-assessable penalty.
hypernym(s): non-assessable lawsuit (preferred), private lawsuit (preferred),
Status: preferred
